Salman Khan

Residence

Khan lives in Bandra, Mumbai, in the Galaxy Apartments. Additionally, he owns a 150-acre estate in Panvel that features three cottages, a gym, and a swimming pool.

Family

Rashid Abdul Salim Salman Khan is the firstborn child of Salim Khan, a screenwriter, and Sushila Charak, his first wife, who took on the name Salma. On December 27, 1965, Khan was born to a Hindu mother and a Muslim father, and he was raised in both religions.

Schooling

Arbaaz and Sohail, Salman's younger siblings, also completed their education at St. Stanislaus High School in Bandra, Mumbai. He and his younger brother Arbaaz had previously attended The Scindia School in Gwalior for a few years. He enrolled in but later left Mumbai's St. Xavier's College.

Career

In 1988, Khan made his screen debut in a supporting role in the movie Biwi Ho To Aisi. Maine Pyar Kiya (1989), a love family drama directed by Sooraj R. Barjatya, starred him in the main role and went on to become one of the highest-grossing Indian films of that era. He was also nominated for Best Male Debut and received his first nomination for Best Actor at Filmfare. Maine Pyar Kiya was called Prema Paavuraalu in Telugu, Te Amo in Spanish, and When Love Calls in English.
